# Music assets for Reels Factory worker

Tracks are **synthetic placeholder loops** generated by `scripts/generate-music.sh` using ffmpeg lavfi sine tones. They are not sampled from third-party recordings.

For production-quality music, replace files in this folder with royalty-free tracks from:
- [Pixabay Music](https://pixabay.com/music/)
- YouTube Audio Library

Keep filenames aligned with `src/music.ts` (`upbeat_drive.mp3`, etc.) and update BPM/beat markers in that file.
